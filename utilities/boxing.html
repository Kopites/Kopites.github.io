A complete set of padded cells. Just for you.

Letter Boxes

Letter boxes provide padding to the top and bottom of a given element.

Available letter box sizes: 

.u-letter-box--super
.u-letter-box--xlarge
.u-letter-box--large
.u-letter-box--medium
.u-letter-box--small
.u-letter-box--xsmall
.u-letter-box--tiny
.u-letter-box--none
